## English

A GIS migration is not finished when layers appear in the geodatabase.

It is finished when you can prove with evidence:

- Which fields, domains and relationships were preserved.
- Which values were truncated, normalized or rejected.
- Which records became null or out-of-domain.
- Which geometries and coordinates still make sense.
- Whether the process can be repeated without relying on manual memory.

With **SIGAudit Global**, I am organizing a public line of services and digital products for GIS audits, migration QA/QC, Excel reporting, Python/C# automation, ArcGIS, Oracle and technical documentation.

The goal is simple: turn scattered checks into clear, reproducible and presentation-ready technical evidence.
